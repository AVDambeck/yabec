Mystic

Requirements: None

Prime requisite: Wisdom, Intelligence

Health die: d6

Maximum level: 12

Armour: leather

Weapons: staff, sling, long-bow

Languages: Alignment, Common, Deep speech

Mystics are a strange folk, who attune themselves to subtle energies. They receive their teachings through deep meditation, rumored come from entities unlike any seen in this world.

Mantra

While not in combat, you can quietly repeat a magical phrase. Each turn you do this, you gain one mantra. You can have up to ten mantra at any given time.

Upon reaching 7th level, you gain two mantra each turn, to a maximum of twenty five.

Mystic Powers

At first level, you learn two mystic powers at random. At each odd level after that, you learn one more at random.

  1. Darkvision: Costs 1 mantra. Gain infravision for one turn.
  2. Divination: Costs 3 mantra. You magically receive an important detail. Choose three topic, and the dungeon master will reveal information about one of them.
  3. Forewarning: Costs 3 mantra. Allow another player to reroll one die.
  4. Precognition: Costs 3 mantra. Preform a search instantly.
  5. Pressure Point: Costs 3 mantra. When you hit, cause the target to be bad until the end of the round.
  6. Psychic Blast: Costs 10 mantra. All other creatures within the same zone as you take Lvd8 damage (save breath for half). You pass out and are bad for the rest of the turn.
  7. Relocate: Costs 7 mantra. Walk to an adjacent zone. To observers, this appears instant. Additionally you move as though under freedom of movement.
  8. Share Lung: Costs 7 mantra. Cast cure wounds.

After Reaching 9th Level

A mystic may establish or build a monastery.

Once a monastery is established, the cleric will attract followers (2d6 mystics of level 1–3, and 2d10 fighters of level 1-2).
