Resist Magical Healing

You can't be magically healed, except from potions.

Endurance

Your maximum endurance is equal to your level plus one. At the end of each turn, if you did not expend endurance that turn, you gain one endurance. Once per turn, you can expend one endurance to preform a warrior skill.

Hollow Skills

At first level, you learn one skill. Each level after that, you learn one additional skill, or advance one skill.

  • Parry: change a hit against you to a miss. Advanced: additionally, you can make a melee attack against the attacker.
  • Janitor: open a door silently. Advanced: this turn and neext, you can not be surprised.
  • Freedom of movement: Effortlessly move up to one zone or 40 feet. You can break out of mundane restraints, climb sheer surfaces, jump over 10' gaps, and ignore difficult terrain and encumberance. Advanced: maintain these powers until the end of the turn.
  • Tough: Gain advantage on dismemberment. Advanced: automaticlally roll a 20 on dismemberment.
  • Echo: when a spellcaster of a lower level than you casts a spell, you can attempt to cast the same spell. To do so, you must check your intelligence. Advanced: you can attempt when a spellcaster of level up to two higher than your own.

    • Stronghold

    Any time a Hollow wishes (and has sufficient money), they can build a castle or stronghold and control the surrounding lands.
