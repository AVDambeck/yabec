Class

A class is an adventuring profession. This page contains a full list of classes, split into three catagories, Basic, Expert, and Demihuman.

Basic

Cleric:

A monastic traveler who can turn undead.

Fighter:

A brave warrior trained in combat.

Thief:

A cunning scout with a variety of skills.

Wizard:

A frail sage who casts spells.

Advanced

Acrobat

A nimble preformer with a multitude of tricks.

Bard

An enchanting minstral who sings.

Druid

A follower of the old gods, who has strange powers.

Knight

An honorable soldire, sworn to a liege.

Mystic

A wander with uncanny powers.

Princess

An intelligant noble who carries royal status.

Warlock

A sinister magician who's made a deal.

Demihuman

Animalkin

A template class that covers any kind of animal.

Dwarf

A stout mountain dweller, that values craftsmanship.

Elf

Fae demihumans that live hundreds of years.

Halfling

A homely creature that is taken on an adventure to destroy a ring.

Kobold

A pathetic dog.
