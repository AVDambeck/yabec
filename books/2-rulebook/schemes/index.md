# Phases
%INDEX%

A *scheme* is a model for how guide the conversation.

There are five *schemes*. Each is detailed fully in it's own chapter.
### Town
Players navigate an urban setting, often splitting up to do *downtime*, or go shopping. The *scheme* ends when the players *depart* on an *adventure*.
### Delve
Players explore a dangerous dungeon. This *scheme* ends when the players leave the dungeon. Then *clean up*.
### Conflict
Players fight or flee from monsters. This *scheme* occers within a *delve*, or rarely, in a *town*.
### Challenge
Players race to get enought *successes*. This *scheme* is blended in with others.
### Clean Up 
Handle experience, splitting treasure, and other loose ends. Either end the session here, or begin *town* again.
