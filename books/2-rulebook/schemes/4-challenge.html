Challenge

Challenges are another scheme the Dungeon Master can employ.

Objective

At the start of a challenge, the Dungeon Master will announce the number of successes needed to complete the challenge.

Checks

  1. Players make action to confront the challenge, and how they will support each other.
  2. Dungeon Master will say which Ability to use, and if it is risky.
  3. The player makes the check, with advantage for each supporter.

Risky

Risky checks start with two disadvantages and failure will remove two successes.
