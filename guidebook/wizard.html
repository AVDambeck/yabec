Arcane Magic

See Magic for full details.

Spell Book: Wizards must carry a spell book to use magic. Wizards begin the game with their first spellbook.

Spell casting: Wizards carry spell books containing the formulae for arcane spells. The table shows the number they may memorize, determined by the character’s experience level. Their spell list is found on Magic p12.

A wizard may build a stronghold, often a great tower. 1d6 apprentices of levels 1–3 will then arrive to study under the wizard.
