#+title: Masscombat

- design goal: resolve warefare quickly and return to dungeon.
- Units can have at most 10 members.
- Total the number of HD in the unit. Note 50%.
- Unit AP equals:
  - one for each member,
  - one for each magic weapon,
  - one for each other magic item on the leader.
- damage: use most common weapon damage die. ignore other properties.
  - conversion: d4, d6, d8 = 1. d10, d12 = 2
  - Multiply by AP
- armor: reduce damage
  - some armor: 1
  - plate and shield: 3
- time: symultanious
- on first blood:
  - check morale. 1-in-6 chance that the leader has to save or be slain
- on 50%:
  - leader savees or is slain with half the group
