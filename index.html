Y.A.B.E.C.

    • Coming from another game?

    If you are familiar with B/X, OSE, or other OSR games, you are already largely familiar with Yabec! The design goals of Yabec are to be modern, and simple. Here's some of the highlight:

    • It's a point-crawl instead of a hex-crawl.
    • Fighters get some unique abilities, and there are bards and princesses.
    • There are fuzzy "zones" instead of exact range measurements.
    • Players, rather than the dungeon master, roll monster attacks as a "dodge roll".
    • Hazards are on a die roll rather than a clock.

    Get Involved!

    Yabec is availble on Github. If theres an issue or a change you'd like to see, bring it up. I'm happy to discuss any aspect of this system with you.
