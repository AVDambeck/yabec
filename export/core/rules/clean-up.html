Divide treasure

Players split up the treasure between them (and their minions if agreed).

Money is typically split evenly, while magic items have conflicting calls diced or bargined for.

Town

Evaluate Loyalty

At the end of each session the hirelings evaluate their loyalty.
- If their boss chooses to pay their wages again, they gain one point. - If an animal or monster got to eat its favorite prey, they gain one point. - If a hireling died that session, non-monster hirelings loose one point. - If a hireling or monster completed a goal, they gain one point. - They may gain or loose more points at the refereeâ€™s discretion.
