Alchemist

Can create potions based on a sample or recipie. See Magical Research. Can research new potions, but takes twice as long and costs twice as much.

Animal Trainer

Can handle upto six mundane animals, or one magical animal.

Assistant

A specialist can have upto two assistants. Assistants are half as effective.

Blacksmith

Creates metal objects, including weapons and armor, at a rate of 100 cp per month.

Engineer/Architect

Expert in planning and managing construction. Required to build strongholds. One Engineer needed per 1,000,000 cp of construction.

Navigator

A ship or party with a navigator will never become lost by mundane means .

Sailor

Trained to run rowed or sailing ships. Amature fighters.

Ship Captain

Seasoned sailer which can command a larger ship.

Spy

Gathers information on a person or group. Chance of success is 100% for unsuspecting groups, 3-in-6 for disconcerning groups, and 1-in-6 for hostile groups.
