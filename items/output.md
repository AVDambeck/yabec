#### Backpack 
 Wearing a backpack grants five additional *slots*, while occupying no slot. Wear only one at a time. 
#### Bandage 
 Used to treat injuries. 
#### Caltrops (1 bag) 
 One bag covers a 10' diamtere. Crossing deals 1d4 damage and reduces movement. 
#### Chalk 
 Makes temporary marks. 10 uses. 
#### Holy Symbol 
 Divine spellcasters are required to carry a symbol of their diety. 
#### Iron Spikes (5) 
 Can be used with mallot to anchor ropes or hold doors. 
#### Karosene (1 flask) 
 Used to fuel lanterns, and as a fire bomb. 
#### Lighter 
 Lights flammable material. 
#### Mallot 
 Can be used to drive spikes. 
#### Mirro 
 Reflective surface. Useful for looking around corners and redirecting gaze attacks. 
#### Pole (10') 
 2" thick, 10' long. Made of wood. Useful for probing. 
#### Ration 
 Preserved food for long journeys. 
#### Rope (50') 
 Can hold the weight of three humans. 
#### Sack 
 Can hold 100 coins or gems. 
#### Thieves' Tools 
 Leath pouch containing various tools including: lock picks, lever bar, shims, file, twine, narrow scissors, pliers, wire 
#### Torches (5) 
 Burns for about 6 *turns*.
