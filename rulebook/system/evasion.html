Evasion

When two groups encounter one another, one or both may decide to evade the other, or one group may decide to purse the evading group.

Decision

If both groups choose to evade, nothing happens.

If one group chooses to evade, and they are not pursed, nothing happens.

If one group chooses to evade, and is pursed, the chance of successfuly evading is 3-in-6. If they fail, begin combat

If one group is hidden from the other and choses to evade, they succeed automatically, and without alerting them.
