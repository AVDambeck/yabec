Schemes

You can think of a scheme as a sub-game, or as a phase the conversation goes through.

  1. Town: Prepare for adventure, make characters, and buy supplies.
  2. Travel: Travel a small overworld to a dungeon.
  3. Explore: Delve into a dungeon, in search of treasure.
  4. Combat: Fight your way out if you have to.
  5. Clean Up: End the session by handle experience, splitting treasure, hosting funerals, and other loose ends.
  6. Challenge: Overcome a large obstacle by succeeding many checks.
  7. Negotiation: Persuade a powerful NPC.
