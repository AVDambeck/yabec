Structures

Players with large amounts of money and power will begin building or buying larger structures. Unless noted otherwise, all costs are in sb.

Construction Crew

Construction requires one engineer per 1,000 sb of construction, as well as a construction site. Towns will have a small construction site, capable of building 1 sb per week. More powerful construction sites can be built, see below.

Seige Weapons

Each seige weapon requires eight people or two horses to move. Additionally, they can't be effectivly aimed at individuals, except for very large monsters.

Ballista20

A very large cross bow. Requires three people to operate, and fires once each round, has a AV of 17, and deals 3d8 damage.

Trebuchet40

Uses a counterweight to fling massive stones. Requires three people to operate, and fires once every other round, has a AV of 19, and deals 5d12.

Screw20

Used to bore through walls. Requires eight people to operate, and fires once each round, has a AV of 15, and deals 4d8.

Sow10

Portable roof used to deflect ranged attacks. Has an AC of 5 and 50 HP.
