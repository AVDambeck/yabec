Players with large amounts of money and power will begin building or buying larger structures. Unless noted otherwise, all costs are in sb.

Construction

Permission

Players need permission from local rulers to undertake construction. For siege weapons and water vessels, this may be a simple request of fee for a permit. For buildings, thie may involve the complex process of aquiring land rights. When constructing in the wilderness, players instead face legistical challenges.

Clear Land

If building in the wilderness, all monsters in the area must be killed or driven off.

Design

The player creates a plan for what they would like to build, and calculates the construction cost. Then seeks approval from the dungeon master.

Costs and Time

Construction requires one engineer per 1,000 sb of construction, as well as a construction site. Towns will have a small construction site, capable of building 1 sb per week. A propper construction site costs 100 sb to build, and 1 sb per week to maintain, but builds at 50 sb per week.
