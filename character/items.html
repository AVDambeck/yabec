Weapons

  • Blunt: Can be used to incapacitate. Required for Clerics.
  • Fired: Makes ranged attacks. Consumes ammo.
  • Fragile: After dealing the maximum damage, the weapon takes 1 damage.
  • Heavy: Takes two slots, and requires two hands to use.
  • Melee: Can be used to make melee attacks.
  • Reach: Melee attacks at close range, and able to reach through allies.
  • Small: Stacks five to a slot.
  • Slow: After making an attack, you can't attack on the next round.
  • Splash: Damages many targets in an area.
  • Thrown: Makes ranged attack by throwing the weapon.
