Kobold

Requirements: Chaotic.

health die: d4

Armour: Leather, Chainmail, Shield

Weapons: non-two-handed

Languages: Alignment, Kobold, 1-in-10 chance each of goblin, orcish, and dark elvish.

Prime requisite: Strength & Charisma

Maximum level: 8

Kobold are canid creatures that dwell in caves. They stand 3’-4’, but often walk on all fours.

Monstrous: Kobold are monstrous. See p5.

Kobold Skills

Kobold have the following skills.

  • Keen Smell: Kobold have a +1 to check doors. Kobold are able to track via sent.
  • Infravision: Kobold have infravision
  • Hiding: Kobold have the ability to avoid detection, apparently vanishing from sight: In woods or undergrowth, a kobold can hide with a 90% chance of success. In dungeons, a kobold can hide in normal lighting conditions with a 2-in-6 chance of success. There must be some form of cover (e.g. shadows) and the kobold must remain motionless and silent.
  • Nature’s Favor: Kobold get +1 healing when receiving healing from a druid or warlock, including bandaging
  • Speak with Dogs: Kobold are able to speak with dogs and other canine as though by a Speak with Animals spell.
  • Natural Weapon: Kobold can use their bite as a natural weapon. It has a d4 damage die, and melee

Pack Leader

Starting at 2nd level, a kobold can draw other kobold to its side. When enemy kobolds fail a moral check, there is an X-in-8 chance they are willing to become your hirelings, where X is your level. Once per session, when you do this, you can attempt a charisma check to increase you renown by one, to a max of 8.

After reaching 5th level

A kobold can take over a cave as a dwelling. 3d6 kobold swear loyalty to the new chieftain.
