Resist Magical Healing

You can't be magically healed, except from potions.

Infravision

You have infravision.

Endurance

Your maximum endurance is equal to your level plus one. At the end of each turn, if you did not expend endurance that turn, you gain one endurance. Once per turn, you can expend one endurance to preform a hollow skill.

Hollow Skills

At first level, you learn one skill. Each level after that, you learn one additional skill, or advance one skill.

  • Break Repoise: when you hit the target must also make a physical saved or loose any remaining actions this round. Advanced: also make an additional attack.
  • Fein: for one turn you appear completely dead. Ends if you move voluntarially. Advanced: until a shift you appear completely dead while remaining motionless.
  • Janitor: open a door silently. Advanced: this turn and next, you can not be surprised.
  • Nerve: this turn reduce any damage from mundane inanimate objects (e.g. spikes) by 3.
  • Tough: Gain advantage on dismemberment. Advanced: automaticlally roll a 20 on dismemberment.
  • Parry: change a hit against you to a miss. Advanced: additionally, you can make a melee attack against the attacker.
  • Solo: while not wearing armor, and in single combat you have a -6 bonus to AV. Advanced: while also wearing a pot, you have an AC of 2.
  • Void: cure a mundane illneess or neutralize a mundane poison. Advanced: transfer a magical poison, illness, or curse to yourself.

Poison Resistence

You are immune to mundane poisons and illness, and have advantage on saving throws against magical poisons and illnesses.
